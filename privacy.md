# Privacy Policy

InfiniBot is a browser extension designed to automate gameplay in Infinite Craft.

## Data Collection
We believe in absolute privacy. InfiniBot **does not collect, store, or transmit** any personal data, usage statistics, or gameplay information to any external servers.

## Local Storage
All game data (such as discovered combinations) is stored exclusively on your local machine using your browser's IndexedDB. This data never leaves your device.

## Third Parties
InfiniBot does not integrate with any third-party analytics or tracking services.
